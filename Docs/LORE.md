## The Great Severance (400 Years Ago)

### The Fall of Aethelgard
Four hundred years ago, **Orodia** was ruled by four great nations. At its heart stood **Aethelgard**, the crown jewel of civilization‚Äîa nation where science and the arcane intertwined in ways the other kingdoms could only dream of.

In their hubris, the Aethelgard Magi-Scientists attempted to pierce the fabric of reality itself, seeking to harvest infinite energy from beyond the veil. They succeeded in opening a doorway. They failed to close it.

The result was **The Severance**. Aethelgard didn't merely explode‚Äîit was *overwritten*. A dimension of chaotic physics called **The Void** bled through, corrupting everything it touched. The kingdom's millions died in seconds. But the dying wasn't the worst part. The worst part was what came after.

## The Player

### Origin: Null-Born
You are a **Null-Born**‚Äîa 5th-generation mutant. You have never seen the sky without the violet shimmer of the Void. You don't remember Aethelgard. You don't care about the history.

You only know:
- The "Iron-Bearer People" (The Triad) have good loot and better food
- The Zone is home, dangerous as it is
- Your mutations are a gift and a curse
- Survival is the only law

### Player Origins (Character Creation)

| Origin | Bonus | Drawback |
|--------|-------|----------|
| **Null-Born** | Balanced stats, stable mutations | No special advantages |
| **First-Gen** | Higher starting stats | Unstable mutations, sanity issues |
| **Gene-Elder Child** | +Reputation with Changed | Expected to lead, enemies target you |
| **Void-Touched** | Powerful mutations, Dark Science affinity | Low starting sanity |
| **Triad Defector** | Tech skills, starts with implant | Hunted by former faction |
| **Scavenger-Born** | +Barter, +Scavenging | Lower combat stats |
| **Cult Initiate** | Dark Science mastery | Distrusted by tribal Changed |

## Dual Path System

### ü¶æ Cybernetics (Tinker Science)
Embrace technology stolen from The Triad. Replace mutated flesh with chrome and circuits.

**Pros:**
- Reliable, consistent bonuses
- No sanity cost
- Can be upgraded with better parts

**Cons:**
- Requires rare tech parts
- EMP vulnerability
- Rejected by some Changed factions

### üß¨ Mutations (Flesh Path)
Embrace the Void. Let your body evolve beyond human limits.

**Pros:**
- Powerful, unique abilities
- Self-regenerating
- Respected by Changed factions

**Cons:**
- Unpredictable (random mutation options)
- Sanity cost for extreme mutations
- Physical appearance changes

### üîÆ Dark Science (Void Path)
Master the corruption itself. Use Void energy to reshape reality.

**Pros:**
- Reality-bending powers
- Craft powerful anomalies
- Access forbidden knowledge

**Cons:**
- Heavy sanity cost
- Void Spawn are drawn to you
- Triad factions will hunt you relentlessly

The ruins are filled with 400-year-old technology more advanced than anything The Triad has today. The Aethelgard Magi-Scientists were centuries ahead‚Äîtheir devices are treasures beyond price.

**Relic Types:**

- **Damaged Relics** - Can be repaired with skill
- **Relic Scrap** - Broken, but materials are useful

## World Events (Rimworld-Style Random Events)

### Neutral Events
- **Void Storm** - Purple storm sweeps the area. Take cover or risk mutation.
- **Trader Caravan** - Syndicate merchants arrive for limited trading.
- **Wandering Mutant** - Friendly Changed seeking shelter.
- **Relic Signal** - Ancient technology activates nearby.

### Hostile Events - Changed
- **Raider Attack** - Hostile mutant raiders assault your position.
- **Beast Migration** - Pack of Mutant Beasts passing through.
- **Hive Swarming** - HiveMother begins spawning event.

### Hostile Events - Triad
- **Sanctum Purge** - Purge Squad sent to cleanse the area.
- **Syndicate Raid** - Slavers hunting for mutant "merchandise."
- **Verdant Collection** - Collectors hunting for "specimens."

### Hostile Events - Void
- **Void Tide** - Reality weakens, Void Spawn emerge.
- **Reality Fracture** - Gravity and time go haywire temporarily.

### Positive Events
- **Cache Discovered** - Hidden supply cache found nearby.
- **Friendly Scavengers** - Changed willing to trade.
- **Anomaly Bloom** - Void Shards crystallize in the area.
- **Ancient Broadcast** - Clue to a nearby Aethelgard vault.

## Naming Conventions

### Changed Names
Descriptive, earned through deeds or mutations:

Formal, bureaucratic:
- Designation-Number (Sanctum): "Operative 7-Alpha"

- Religious names (Verdant): "Brother/Sister [Virtue]" - "Sister Clarity"

Ominous, void-themed:
